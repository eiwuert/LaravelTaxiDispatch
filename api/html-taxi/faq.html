Q. What are the services offered by MOBYCABS?

You can book MOBYCABS on our MOBYCABS App for Current as well as Advance Bookings upto 1 month. For current bookings, just click on 'Taxi Now' button & once your booking is confirmed, the Reference Number and Driver details are displayed. In case of advance booking, the confirmation is given right away and cab details are sent 20 minutes before the pick-up time.

Q. What are the timings for the MOBYCABS Service?

MOBYCABS Services are available round the clock.

Q. How do I book a MOBYCABS?

You can book a MOBYCABS either through Phone or MOBYCABS Mobile Application or Website.

  • To book through phone, please call 4050607
  • To book MOBYCABS instantly, download MOBYCABS Mobile Application on Android Phone or iPhone
  • To book a MOBYCABS through the website, visit our home page: http://mobycabs.co.in/

Q. Can I hail a MOBYCABS cab on the road?

No, you cannot hail a MOBYCABS Cab on the road. You can book a Taxi through Phone, App or Website.

Q. Is there a charge for booking on the phone / web?

No, there is no 'Convenience Charge' for booking a Taxi if you book through our call center number or through our website.

Q. What is the fare that I will be charged?

We charge fare on the basis of the actual distance travelled in kilometers and the actual wait time in minutes along with the Ride time charges. The fares are in line with the fare guidelines prescribed by the Regional Transport Authority / State Transport Authority, wherever applicable. MOBYCABS Cabs believes in Fixed and Transparent Pricing Policy.

Q. Will I be charged for waiting time also?

Yes you will charged the waiting time also.

Q. Do I have to pay toll charge when I travel through MOBYCABS?

  • Yes, you will be paying 'Toll charge'- at the actual,, when you are crossing the toll post while traveling through MOBYCABS.
  • However, in case you have booked a Taxi & the cab is crossing the toll post while coming to pick you up, then you need not pay the toll charges.

Q. How do I pay after the completion of my trip?

MOBYCABS accepts both Cash and Cashless payments i.e. you can pay via Cash as well as via Mobile Wallet after completion of your trip with MOBYCABS. For Wallet Payments, Load your wallet at Payments Section and once the ride is completed the applicable trip amount will be directly deducted from your respective chosen Wallet.

Q. Will I get a printed receipt for the amount paid?

Of course you will. You will receive the trip receipt immediately on your email ID at the end of every trip without having to follow up for the same. You may also view your trip receipts (only belonging to app bookings) on the app in Past Bookings Section

Q. Can I pay using a debit or a credit card?

We don’t have any option to pay directly via debit or a credit card, but with few simple steps you can load your wallet using Credit/Debit card and start using MOBYCABS’s Wallet feature for all your rides 

Q. Money got debited from bank account but not credited to my MOBYCABS Wallet. How will I solve this issue?

Generally, this happens when the communication from bank/payment gateway wasn't received at the time of payment. In such cases, we receive a confirmation from the bank/payment gateway later and then we initiate a refund for the payment to your account.

You will receive an email from us notifying you for the same. This generally takes about 3-5 days for credit/debit card transaction and 7-8 days in case of net banking.

Q. What do I do if I have left something behind in a MOBYCABS?

Please call our Customer Service Centre at 4050607 and give us the details of the cab number, date and time of travel (all this information will be there on your printed fare receipt / e-receipt) and we will help you track down your missing item. MOBYCABS chauffeurs are trained to report items left behind in a cab. So if you tell us the details of your commute, it makes the job easy for us to link you with your missing item!

Q. Can I cancel a booking made?

Our system of handling bookings is automated and once your journey is confirmed by our Customer Service Center, a cab is assigned to report to you. We do understand that sometimes, plans change and you may need to cancel a booking. Please tell us as soon as you reasonably can - that you would like to cancel a booking so that we can avoid a MOBYCABS reporting to you, when you don't need it.

Q. In which cities is the MOBYCABS Service currently available?

The MOBYCABS cab service is now available in Coimbatore. We expect to start operations in other major cities soon.
